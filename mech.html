Mechanical Engineering

About Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering is a branch of engineering that applies principles of physics, mathematics, and material science to design, analyze, manufacture, and maintain mechanical systems. It plays a crucial role in various industries, from automotive to aerospace.
