Civil Engineering - Year 1

About Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering is a discipline that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure projects such as buildings, roads, bridges, and more. Year 1 provides a foundation in core engineering principles.

Syllabus - Year 1

  • Calculus
  • Linear Algebra
  • Probability and Statistics
  • Introduction to Civil Engineering Concepts
  • History and Significance
  • Statics
  • Strength of Materials
